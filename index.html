Who I am

Ph. D. Candidate at the University of Zaragoza

I received the Bachelor in Electronics, Robotics an Mechatronics Engineering from the University of Malaga and the Master's in Robotics, Graphics and Computer Vision from the University of Zaragoza in 2020 and 2022 respectively. I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Systems Engineering at the University of Zaragoza, funded by a FPU national grant. My current reserach interests include active pereception, semantic scene understanding, drone control and multi-robot collaboration.
